KIKWETU RESTAURANT

Kikwetu Restaurant is a high class restaurant that offers wide range of dishes ranging from local dishes to foreign dishes. It is located at the outskirts of Nakuru town along Nakuru-Ravine highway, approximately 5kilometres from Nakuru. It boasts of highly qualified staff members and managers who are result oriented and very much concerned with their customers' satisfaction.
